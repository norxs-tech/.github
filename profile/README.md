# norxs Technology LLC

norxs delivers end-to-end engineering, functional safety, and cybersecurity solutions for safety-critical systems. We integrate hardware design, embedded firmware development, and certification pathways into a unified engineering lifecycle, ensuring strict compliance and reliability from architectural concept to production.

## Engineering Expertise

* **Functional Safety Engineering:** ASIL-D safety lifecycle execution (ISO 26262, IEC 61508) for high-voltage SiC/IGBT traction inverters and powertrain systems.
* **Automotive Cybersecurity:** Comprehensive threat analysis and risk assessment (TARA), CSMS implementation, and threat modeling per ISO/SAE 21434 and UN R155/R156.
* **Embedded Systems & Control:** ASPICE-aligned software development (ISO 26262-6), focusing on PMSM motor control and power distribution unit (PDU) firmware.
* **Power Electronics Design:** Hardware architecture, PCB design, and V&V for traction inverters, onboard chargers (OBC), and battery management systems (BMS).
